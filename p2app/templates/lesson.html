Follow the video guide

To connect wix-domains.com to your site, complete the following 5 steps in this interactive video guide.

1/5

Sign in to GoDaddy

  • Go to GoDaddy

  • Sign in to you account using your GoDaddy username and password

2/5

Go to DNS Nameservers

  • At the top right corner of the page,
    click on your Username

  • Click on My Products

  • Find [yourdomain.com] and click on DNS

3/5

Change the Nameserver type

  • Scroll down to the Nameservers section

  • Click Change

  • Select Custom from the drop-down menu

4/5

Add the Wix Nameserves

  • Copy each of the Wix Nameservers, individually

  • ns0.wixdns.net Copy

  • ns1.wixdns.net Copy

  • Paste each one of the Wix Nameservers in the text boxes

  • Click Save
